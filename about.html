Our Core Principles

Integrity

Truth is elusive, but we pursue it.
We strive to deliver accurate,
meaningful and honest products.

Partnership

The best solutions will arise by
mixing our expertise with yours.
We suggest starting consulting
engagements with training, to
strengthen our collaboration.

Communication

We do our best to anticipate your
needs for information, and
reach out with appropriate
detail and frequency.

Cost Effectiveness

We are accustomed to tight budgets. If we
think of a lower-cost way to achieve your
goals, while still maintaining quality,
we will suggest it.

Our Story

Crow Insight was founded in 2014 to help organizations aiming to make a lasting impact on the world. Our founder Mike Crow had been working in program development and evaluation, helping agencies set goals and develop key performance indicators. He knew these organizations could make better decisions and more easily share their stories if they knew how to visualize and convey their data, and he knew he could help them. Since our founding, we've grown into a small multidisciplinary team with remote offices around the United States.

Working At Crow Insight

As a team of curious, creative, flexible collaborators, we value the individual perspective each team member brings. We constantly solicit employee suggestions on ways to enhance quality, teamwork and efficiency. We invest in research and professional development, allowing even our most junior team members to be more deeply involved in proposing and developing effective client solutions.
