Our Core Principles

Integrity

Truth is elusive, but we pursue it. We strive to deliver accurate, meaningful and honest products.

Partnership

The best solutions will arise by mixing our expertise with yours. We suggest starting consulting engagements with training, to strengthen our collaboration.

Communication

We do our best to anticipate your needs for information, and reach out with appropriate detail and frequency.

Cost Effectiveness

We are accustomed to tight budgets. If we think of a lower-cost way to achieve your goals, while still maintaining quality, we will suggest it.

Our Story

Crow Insight was founded in 2013 to help organizations aiming to make a lasting impact on the world. Our founder Mike Crow had been working in program development and evaluation, helping public-sector agencies set goals and measure progress. He believed these organizations could make better decisions and more easily share their stories if they knew how to effectively visualize and convey their data, and he made it his mission to help them. Since our founding, we've grown into a small multidisciplinary team with remote offices around the United States.

Our Team
