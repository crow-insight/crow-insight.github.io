Develop Deeper Understanding

We strive to ensure that we have a deep understanding of the context, information, data and messages that need to be communicated. Shortly after project start, in preparation for a kickoff meeting, we will begin working with the project liaison to gather and review all needed information. We will identify and communicate essential questions to the project liaison and/or the project team and experts.

Identify Requirements

A successful project requires establishing, adapting and meeting requirements. For this project, requirements will operate at several levels.

Design, Iterate, & Polish

Depending upon the design complexity involved, each product will involve various steps of design, iteration, and polish.
